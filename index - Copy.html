    Dosage & Instructions

    For oral administration. Shake well before use.


    Adult and children over 12 years: 10-20 mL after meals and at bedtime, up to four times a day.

    Children 6 to 12 years: 5-10 mL after meals and at bedtime, up to four times a day.

    Children under 6 years: Not recommended.

    Elderly: No dosage modification is required for this age group.

    FAQs

    What is Gaviflux?

    Gaviflux is designed to treat Heartburn & Acid Reflux. Gaviflux relief the symptoms within 3 minutes.


    What are the side effects?

    Gaviflux is made using all-natural seaweed. Side effects are very rarely reported. If you do experience any side effects they are highly likely to be mild and to go away when you stop taking Gaviflux.

    Speak to your doctor if you experience:

  • Constipation
  • Diarrhoea
  • Nausea
  • Stomach Cramps
  • Taste Impairment
  • Vomiting

    • Is it safe to take Gaviflux in pregnant woman?

    Gaviflux is safe for pregnant & breastfeeding mother. It starts working in 1 minute and provides long-lasting relief (up to 4 hours).

    How Gaviflux works:

    Gaviflux starts action with Sodium Alginate. Refined from brown seaweed which is sustainably harvested, this effective formulation acts as a protective barrier against Acid Reflux & Heartburn and provides faster relief.

    When the Sodium Alginate comes into contact with the irritating gastric acid, the Alginate forms “raft”, which acts as a protective barrier over stomach contents, stopping acid from rising and soothing the burning sensation in the chest.

    Sodium Bicarbonate and Calcium Carbonate act as Acid Neutralizers, countering the problematic stomach acid. The protective barrier or raft lasts for up to 4 hours.
